What Makes
a True Challenger
Here's Our
Checklist.

We're looking for brands with grit, guts and a mission. See if you fit the bill.


To us, a Challenger brand isn’t defined by its size, but by its spirit. Here’s what we’re looking for. If you see your brand here, we’re ready to make things happen.

You’re here to lead, to dominate, and to create something bigger than yourself. You see a future that’s different from today—and you’re ready to make it happen.

You’re not held back by how things ‘should’ be done. You ask questions others won’t, and you’re not afraid to disrupt norms to find new solutions. Fresh takes are your superpower.

You know who you are and what you stand for. You’re here to be a beacon for something that matters, and you’re unshakeable in your beliefs. Your brand is a rallying point for millions, not just a product.

You’re willing to cut the fluff and focus on what makes the biggest impact. You choose what to do—and what to leave behind. You’re ready to go all-in where it counts, even if that means sacrificing elsewhere.

You don’t just tell customers what you sell. You pull them into a story that they want to be part of. You’re ready to spark curiosity, build loyalty, and create fans, not just customers.

Safe and predictable isn’t your style. You’re ready to step into the unknown and make bold moves, even if that means going against the grain. Calculated risks are where you thrive.

You don’t have to be the biggest or the richest to win. You know how to make the most of what you’ve got, and you’re not afraid to hustle. You’re scrappy, creative, and unstoppable.

You’re not looking to fit in. You’re here to change the game, make waves, and shake things up. You believe in doing things differently—and you’re committed to showing the world why that matters.

About Us

Founded in August 2024, Lighthouse Brands is a direct marketing and sales agency for startup and emerging online retail brands.

We help bold online retail brands in fashion, beauty, education, toys, and home goods in Asia, Europe and North America take on the giants.

We take on the heavy lifting with a full-service, skin-in-the- game approach— handling all the boring and tedious operational stuff so you, the brand owner can focus on what you’re best at, like product design and community engagement—backed by tailored strategy, seamless operations, and data- driven growth for win-win results that help brands break through and thrive.
